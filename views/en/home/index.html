Add Suivix

What is that?

Suivix is a Discord bot developed in Javascript.
This bot allows you to keep track of the presence of certain people on Discord by sending you a report. The bot is available in English and French!
Warning, Suivix needs your Discord server to be equipped with a minimum of a voice channel and a role.

This bot uses an online interface to make your experience more pleasant, simple and efficient. Thanks to the use of cookies you stay connected between each attendance request.
Exemple of a report here.

For more information use !suivix help

Try Suivix

How does it work?

Create a request

By typing !suivix in a text channel on Discord (having previously added Suivix to your server), you create an attendance request.

Choose your options

After creating an attendance request, click on the proposed link (redirection to suivix.xyz).Then, using the graphical interface, select the different options (a voice channel and a role) that will be used to define your result.

Come back to Discord

Yes... It's already over! Go back to Discord to see the result of your attendance.
